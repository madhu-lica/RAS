Welcome to Resource Allocation System

The RAS aims at automating the process of allocation and optimal management of the resources available in the CSE department of the SSN college of engineering. The system can be used by Students and members of the Faculty with their login credentials. Students can use the system to view the list of resources allocated and faculty members can use it to reserve the resources in advance. This system serves its purpose by displaying the information about the list of resources, their availability and usage.
